Class Departments

Enver Creek's classes are all taught by outstanding teachers, and the classes offer many different paths for each department.

Math

Enver Creek's Math department has teachers that will help students understand the curriculum they have to learn for the current grade. They are willing to help students outside of school, like the majority of teachers in Enver Creek.

English

The English department has students learn skills for university, with other units being taught for specific career paths like poetry.

Social Studies

The Social Studies department has students learn key events in the history of Canada, the United States and in general the world.
