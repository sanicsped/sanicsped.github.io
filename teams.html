Clubs and Teams

Enver Creek's clubs and teams are very diverse and with the amount of clubs, there is bound to be atleast one club that is interesting to a student.

Robotics

Enver Creek Secondary's Robotics club represents creativity and critical thinking. It is a very interesting club and is one of the most prestigious clubs in the school.

Soccer

Enver Creek Secondary's soccer club is a great club to join to be physically active. It is very athletic and it offers entertaining soccer matches with other schools.

Chess

The Chess club in Enver Creek is one that is very active and many students engage in chess. It makes students think critically to defeat their opponent with strategic moves each and every game.
